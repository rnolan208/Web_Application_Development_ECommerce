ABOUT


Welcome to Robert’s Football Shop, your first stop for football merchandise from top football leagues.


Robert’s Football Shop is an e-commerce store created as part of the Web Applications Development module within the Software Development H. Dip. at ATU 2025/26.
The website was designed to showcase the core features and functionality of a modern online retail platform. The store specialises in football apparel and equipment, with a strong focus on quality, variety, and ease of use.
My aim is to provide a smooth and enjoyable shopping experience, from browsing products to completing secure online purchases, while applying real-world HTML, CSS, JavaScipt, and NODE principles learned through academic study.

We offer a wide selection of soccer jerseys from top competitions, including:


  • Premier League
  • League of Ireland
  • La Liga
  • Serie A
  • Bundesliga
  • Ligue 1
  • International Teams

In addition to jerseys, Robert’s Football Shop also stocks a range of football essentials, such as footballs, goalkeeper gloves, soccer goals, and shin guards.
